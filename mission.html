OUR MISSION
Cancer is indiscriminate. It can affect anyone, in any place, at any time. With parents in the medical field, we hear stories of the loss
that cancer brings with it. Cancer ruins careers, leaves long-lasting psychological trauma, and takes our loved ones from us.
Yet, so few people truly understand how cancer works, how to prevent it, and how to go about requesting medical assistance.
Screening on Wheels is designed to provide underserved communities with all of this, so as to work to stop cancer in its tracks.
