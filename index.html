THE STORY BEHIND IT ALL
Over the summer, on our trip to Costa Rica, we visited a volunteer clinic for uninsured
Costa Ricans where even simple illnesses could become chronic, deadly conditions.
We were stunned by the lack of access to treatment let alone information; so when we
returned, we set out on a mission to provide those in need with: awareness & screenings.
