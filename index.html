Screening on Wheels

Healing disadvantaged people through cancer pamphlets distributed to local practices.

Get Started

Community Oriented.

Screening on Wheels is a medical advertising company started by Arizonan students. We seek to solve the growing lack of access to healthcare and medicine by providing free cancer screenings. Our mission is financed by selling informational fliers on breast, cervical, skin, and stomach cancer. We sell fliers and sponsorship space to local healthcare practices, allowing them to advertise themselves and help their patients.
